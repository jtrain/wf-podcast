Welcome to Episode #3, we talk about Musselroe commissioning. The largest wind farm in Tasmania.
Part owned by Hydro Tasmania and the chinese company Shenhua.

some quick stats.
- size: 168 MW
- cost: $400M
- commissioned: July 2013
